Chydorid

Chydoridae is a common cladoceran species in the Delta, recognizable by its round body morphology and short rostrum. Chydorids look similar to Bosmina in that both species have a circular body shape, but a Bosmina has a long first antennae extending from its rostrum while a Chydorid does not. Like most other cladoceran, Chydorids are more likely to be found in a body of slow moving water like Wings Landing or other pond sites.
